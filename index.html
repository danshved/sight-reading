This page selects pieces for sight-reading exams based on BachScholar's "Sight-Reading & Harmony". You will be presented six pieces to play: one with a key signature of no sharps or flats, one with one sharp, one with one flat, one with two sharps, one with two flats, and one with three or more sharps or flats. Three of them will be in minor keys and the other three in major keys. The pieces will be presented in random order.

Reloading the page will generate a new sequence each time.
